Campylobacter

(Vibro) Fetus Vaccine

For the prophylactic immunisation of heifers and bulls against bovine vibriosis.

Composition: An oil - emulsion of formalin-inactivated Campylobacter fetus veneralis and Campylobacter fetus intestinalis bacteria.

Dosage: Heifers: 2 ml subcutaneously. Bulls: 5 ml subcutaneously.

Reg num: G 0055 (Act 36/1947)

Namibia code: V01/24.4/627

Product code: 1282

Chlamysure

(Enzootic Abortion Vaccine)

For the prophylactic immunisation of cattle, sheep and goats against enzootic abortion.

Composition: Oil-adjuvanted emulsion of formal inactivated cell-adapted cultures of Chlamydophila abortus.

Dosage: Cattle: 2 ml intramuscularly. Sheep & goats: 1 ml subcutaneously.

Reg num: G 3862 (Act 36/1947)

Namibia code: V10/24.4/1184

Product code: 2192

Clostridium Septicum

(Vaccine)

For the immunisation of sheep, goats and cattle against CI. septicum infection.

Dosage: Sheep & goats: 2 ml subcutaneously. Cattle: 5 ml subcutaneously.

Reg num: G 0133 (Act 36/1947)

Namibia code: V01/24.4/143

How to submit an RFQ / Bid

  1. Quantify and qualify your current business profile against all stated requirements.
  2. Register a supplier profile on the nominated portal (see contact/links to the right).
  3. Follow the submission instructions included with each RFQ brief.
  4. Submit company documentation via the our online portal — we do not accept faxed or manual submissions (zero‑carbon policy).
  5. Await confirmation and further instructions; you will be notified of site inspections or next steps where applicable.
!
Important information
Please contact OBP on (012) 522 1500 or email info@obpvaccines.co.za for RFQ clarification. OBP reserves the right to award, forgo awarding, or cancel a scope of work at any phase of the submission period. Site inspections may occur where relevant; prospective bidders will be notified.

Innovation, Research & Development Partners

OBP recognises the Innovation, Research and Development (IR&D) department as the cornerstone for the latest vaccine technologies and innovation to expand our product portfolio, and to cultivate and pursue scientific excellence.

Our partners play an intrinsic role in our IR&D initiatives. OBP relies on its national and international network of collaborators to source new vaccine antigen candidates or diagnostic agents in the upscaling, validation and adoption of new products and technologies.

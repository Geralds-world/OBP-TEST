About

Onderstepoort Biological Products (SOC) Ltd (OBP) is a South African state-owned animal vaccine manufacturing company whose mandate is to prevent and control animal diseases that impact food security, human health and livelihoods.

✓
Mandate focus Develop innovative, affordable and accessible vaccines to protect livestock, food security and livelihoods across the continent.

Our Mandate

The mandate is delivered through continued development of innovative products and efficient manufacturing, which ensures vaccine affordability and accessibility through varied distribution channels to prevent and control animal diseases that impact food security, human health, and livelihoods.

OBP is strategically positioned to promote food security by continuing to support animal health and production, especially applicable to important diseases, which have severe economic impact but may fall outside the product scope of multi-national companies.

Scope
Vaccines for viral, bacterial and protozoal diseases of cattle, sheep, goats and horses.
Distribution
Distributed across Africa, Middle East & Europe.
Reach
Commercial livestock sector, co-ops, wholesalers and rural networks.
Impact
Accessible products and training for small entrepreneurs and farmers.

The OBP product portfolio comprises veterinary vaccines against viral, bacterial, and protozoal diseases of cattle, sheep, goats, and horses, with major relevance to South Africa and Africa. OBP products are distributed in Africa, the Middle East and Europe. We serve the commercial livestock sector, which includes co-ops, and wholesalers throughout the country. Established distribution networks within rural areas make products and training easily accessible to small entrepreneurs.

If you need more detail or supporting documents, contact OBP communications for policy briefs and product literature.

How we deliver

Innovation, quality manufacturing and partnerships ensure vaccine availability and affordability across varied channels.
